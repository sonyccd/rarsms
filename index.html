üìã How It Works

  1. Send a message to RARSMS via APRS from your authorized callsign
  2. Message appears instantly in our Discord channel with your callsign and content
  3. Club members respond using Discord's reply feature to create a threaded conversation
  4. Replies are routed back to your APRS station automatically
  5. View the conversation in the web dashboard for complete message history
